Refund & Cancellation Policy

Reservation Cancellations

Cancellations made at least 4 hours before the reservation time are free of charge. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee if a deposit was required.

Event Deposits

Deposits for private events and catering are refundable if cancelled at least 7 days prior to the event date. Cancellations within 7 days will forfeit the deposit.

Food Quality

If you are unsatisfied with the quality of your meal, please inform our staff immediately. We strive to resolve all issues onsite to ensure your satisfaction.
